**IIT Patna, 2023 – 2025**

Founded the **Research Club at IIT Patna** with the goal of giving undergraduates a low-friction way into research.

What we did:

- Organised **guest talks** by professors and industry researchers.
- Ran **poster sessions** where students from different departments presented ongoing work.
- Curated **reading groups** on ML, systems, and theory.

The club is now an active part of campus life.

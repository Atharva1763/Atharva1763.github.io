**DBMS course project (Prof. Samrat Mondal, IIT Patna) · Apr 2023 – May 2023**

A comprehensive **placement portal** designed around five user roles:

- **Students** — apply, track applications, manage profiles
- **Companies** — post openings, shortlist candidates
- **Alumni** — connect with juniors and mentor
- **TPC officers** — orchestrate the placement season
- **System administrators** — manage users and audits

I designed the **ER diagram** and database tables from scratch and implemented separate UIs/PHP handlers per role.
